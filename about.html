About us

City of Westminster College is one of the top vocational colleges located in Westminster, providing around 250 full-time and part-time courses. Each year around 7,000 students are been enrolled in the college. City of Westminster has two campuses and there are in Paddington and Maida Vale.

The target audience for creating a fake news website is for students who are aged between 16 to 19 years old whether it is a male or a gender. I would also want to show this to students that are doing ESOL courses so that they can be aware of fake news because English is not their first language.

One major objective for creating a ‘Fake News’ website is to educate students in City of Westminster College. This website will help students to identify any fake news and rumours and will be able to see fake news videos and images. Currently, many younger generations tend to believe in any news that is being shown on the website, TV, and newspaper. And this could impact on their lifestyle and how they get on with their daily life.
